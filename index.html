Table Wear

It is proper etiquette to start with the dishes on the left and right sides of the plate and move closer to the plate as the meal progresses. When setting the table, place the dinner fork on the left side of the plate, and place the salad fork on the left side of the dinner fork. The knife is placed directly to the right of the plate, and the spoon is placed next to it.
